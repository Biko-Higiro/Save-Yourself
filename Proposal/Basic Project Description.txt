Time management app and habit builder app.

You Login

You input the habits you want to build and the habits you want to kill.

You input daily time spend.

It analyses your time spend today and in the past

tells you what you need to keep doing, what you need to stop and what you need to improve from day to day.

e.g You spent 3 hours on your phone after eating at 6 try eating without your phone an going for a walk after.
